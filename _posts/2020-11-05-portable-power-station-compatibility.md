#### Output port 
Wind Catcher comes with a XT90 Output port. Included in the kit is the following adapters:
- XT90 to MX4
- XT90 to 8MM DC barrel connector used on Goalzero and Jackery. 

We also sell an optional Anderson Powerpole adapter.
